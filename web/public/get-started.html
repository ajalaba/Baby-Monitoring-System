Baby Monitor System

INTRODUCTION

The BabyMonitor Web Application is a project created by ADV; a group of 2nd year Software Engineering students.

We developed this web application to help parents and caretakers to monitor the health and activity statistics of a baby. So, busy parents can leave their babies in their rooms without worrying too much about accidents occuring.

Instructions for users:-

  • Users can create and login to accounts using the registration and login pages.
  • Registered users can then register their devices and see a list of the devices they have under their name.
  • By clicking on a device in the device list page, the sensor data for the device can be viewed.
  • Visualizations of the sensor data can also be viewed by clicking on the relevant links.
